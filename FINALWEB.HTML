About E-Max Education

E-Max Computer Education has been transforming lives through quality computer education since 2012. Our mission is simple — equip every student with the digital skills needed to thrive in today's technology-driven world.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide affordable, industry-relevant computer education that empowers students from all backgrounds to achieve career success and financial independence through digital skills.

🌟 Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred computer education institute in Uttar Pradesh, known for quality training, student success, and industry connections.

Our Journey

Institute Timeline

2012 — Foundation

E-Max Computer Education Established

Started with a small computer lab of 10 systems and a vision to democratize digital education in Bareilly.

2015 — Expansion

New Campus & 500+ Students

Expanded to a larger facility with 30+ computers and launched advanced courses including Tally and Graphic Design.

2018 — Recognition

Government Affiliation & Awards

Received government recognition and won the Best Computer Institute award in Uttar Pradesh.

2021 — Digital Era

Online Classes & 2000+ Alumni

Launched online learning platform during pandemic, maintaining uninterrupted education for all students.

2024 — Present

5000+ Students Trained

Now the most preferred computer institute in the region with 100% placement record and 50+ hiring partners.
